Compact Standup Pouch Machine

This machine is made with a focus on engineering ingenuity, high quality production standards and international standard components to consistently achieve high production speeds. The standup pouch maker is designed to convert laminated film into standup and three side seal pouches.

This 400mm wide machine is designed to be a value machine for our customers, specifically for jobs in the pouch width range of 100-400mm.

Various attachments such as Insert Mother Film, Insert Gusset, Punches, Round Corner Cut and more, can be equipped with this machine.
